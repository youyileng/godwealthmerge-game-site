App Purpose & Game Description

God Wealth Merge is an engaging casual strategy game designed for mobile devices. The core purpose of the application is to provide entertainment through strategic merging mechanics.

Players summon characters based on the "God of Wealth" theme and merge identical units to evolve them into higher-level forms. The game encourages logical thinking and resource management as players strive to unlock the ultimate character while collecting virtual currency (Gold Coins) within the game environment.

Why use Google Sign-In?

Our game integrates Google Play Games Services to enhance the user experience. We require Google Sign-In for the following specific purposes:

  • Cloud Save (Data Backup): To securely save your game progress (including current level, unlocked characters, and coin balance) to the cloud. This ensures you never lose your progress even if you switch devices or reinstall the app.
  • Leaderboards: To allow you to participate in global rankings and compare your high scores with other players, fostering a fun and competitive community.

Privacy Commitment: We do not access, store, or share your personal information (such as email address, phone number, or contacts) for any purpose other than the core game functionality described above.
